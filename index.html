About Us

ZackFinance is a simple marketplace created to help entrepreneurs share their projects and connect with potential supporters. The platform is designed to be light, fast, and easy to use.

Only the admin can publish projects, ensuring all posted items are verified and safe. Visitors can view projects easily without any sign-up.

We focus on clarity, transparency, and easy communication. If you have a project or want to learn more, feel free to reach out using the contact below.
